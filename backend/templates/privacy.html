Privacy Policy

How we handle your data on AI Audio Generator.

1. Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AI Audio Generator handles the text and audio you create using our platform.

2. Text & Audio Data

The text you enter is processed only for the purpose of generating audio. We do not use your content to train models or share it with third parties.

Generated audio files are stored on our server so that you can play them from your account history. These files may be removed periodically for storage and maintenance reasons.

3. Account Information

When you create an account, we store your username, email, and an encrypted version of your password. We never store plain-text passwords.

4. Cookies & Sessions

We use sessions (and standard cookies) only to keep you logged in and to secure your account while using the site.

5. Third-Party Services

Our text-to-speech feature may rely on third-party AI providers. Your text may be temporarily sent to these services only to generate the audio output.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy over time. Any important changes will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, feel free to contact us via the support section of the website.
